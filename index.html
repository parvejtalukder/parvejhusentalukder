Biography

Who is Parvej Husen Talukder and What Drives Him in the World of Literature, Technology, and Entrepreneurship?

Md Parvej Husen Talukder, mostly known as Parvej Husen Talukder, is a Bangladeshi poet, young adult writer, internet entrepreneur, and former journalist. He founded Kavya Kishor International, the multilingual encyclopedia Bhikitia, and co-founded the wiki-based platform WikiGenius.

Born on August 23, 2005, in Derai Upazila, Sunamganj, Parvej began writing rhymes and poems in childhood. His works quickly gained recognition among young readers in Bangladesh.

In 2021, he was titled "Sultan Rhymester of the Haor region." He received the Jagrata Sahitya Puraskar in 2022 and was featured in the 2023 Chorabarshiki by Bangladesh Shishu Academy.

Parvej is passionate about combining literature and technology to promote accessible learning. Through Bhikitia and Bhikitia Pro, he supports multilingual knowledge sharing.

He is currently studying Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and continues to inspire through his creative and digital initiatives.
