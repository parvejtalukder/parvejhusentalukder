About Parvej Husen

Poet, Writer, Internet Entrepreneur, and Visionary from Bangladesh

Who is Parvej Husen Talukder?

Md Parvej Husen Talukder is a multifaceted Bangladeshi talent - a poet, young adult writer, internet entrepreneur, and former journalist. Born on August 23, 2005, in Derai Upazila, Sunamganj, he has emerged as a prominent voice in contemporary Bengali literature while simultaneously making significant contributions to Bangladesh's digital landscape.

Parvej's literary journey began in childhood when he started composing rhymes and poems. His works quickly gained recognition for their emotional depth and connection with young readers. By his late teens, he had established himself as one of the promising new voices in Bangladeshi poetry.

Parallel to his literary pursuits, Parvej demonstrated remarkable entrepreneurial vision. He founded several digital platforms including Kavya Kishor International (a global literary organization), Bhikitia (a multilingual encyclopedia), and co-founded WikiGenius, showcasing his unique ability to bridge literature and technology.

Currently pursuing Computer Science and Engineering at Sylhet International University, Parvej continues to innovate at the intersection of literature and digital technology, driven by his mission to make knowledge and literary resources more accessible.

Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To bridge the gap between literature and technology by creating platforms that make knowledge and creative expression accessible to all, while preserving and promoting Bengali language and culture in the digital age. Through my writing and digital initiatives, I aim to inspire young minds, foster literary appreciation, and contribute to Bangladesh's growing tech ecosystem.

Vision Statement

I envision a future where technology empowers literary expression and cultural preservation. My goal is to establish Bangladesh as a hub for innovative digital platforms that serve both local and global communities, while nurturing the next generation of writers and tech entrepreneurs. Through sustainable projects, I aim to create ecosystems where literature and technology thrive together, making quality education and creative resources available to everyone regardless of language or location.

My Journey

Early Life & Education

2005-2019

Born in Derai Upazila, Sunamganj. Attended Gachiya Government Primary School and Gachiya Shamsuddin Sikander High School. Began writing poetry and rhymes at an early age, showing exceptional literary talent.

Literary Recognition

2020-2021

Gained recognition as a young poet in regional literary circles. Published works in various literary magazines and platforms. Began receiving invitations to literary events and poetry recitations.

Entrepreneurial Beginnings

2021

Founded Bhikitia, a multilingual encyclopedia project. Established Kavya Kishor International as a platform for young poets and writers. Co-founded WikiGenius, demonstrating early interest in collaborative knowledge platforms.

Awards & Recognition

2022

Honored with the title "Sultan Rhymester of the Haor region" by Ekushey Sangbad.com. Received the prestigious Jagrata Sahitya Puraskar (Jagrata Literature Award) for contributions to Bengali literature.

National Recognition

2023

Featured in the 2023 Chorabarshiki by Bangladesh Shishu Academy, a significant national recognition for children's writers. Continued expansion of digital projects including Bhikitia Pro and Khujoweb search engine.

Current Endeavors

2024-Present

Pursuing Computer Science and Engineering at Sylhet International University. Simultaneously working on expanding Kavya Kishor International's global reach and developing new digital platforms that combine literary and technological innovation.

Notable Achievements

My Philosophy

On Literature

"Poetry is not just words arranged in rhythm; it's the heartbeat of a culture, the whisper of generations, and the bridge between souls across time and space. My writing seeks to capture the essence of Bangladeshi identity while speaking to universal human experiences."

"I believe children's literature should both educate and enchant, planting seeds of curiosity that blossom into lifelong learning. Through my works for young readers, I aim to nurture imagination while grounding stories in our rich cultural heritage."

On Technology

"Technology, when thoughtfully applied, can be the great equalizer - breaking down barriers to education and cultural exchange. My digital projects aim to democratize access to knowledge while preserving linguistic diversity in an increasingly connected world."

"The future belongs to those who can marry technical innovation with humanistic values. As a computer science student, I'm committed to developing solutions that serve our communities while advancing Bangladesh's position in the global digital economy."
